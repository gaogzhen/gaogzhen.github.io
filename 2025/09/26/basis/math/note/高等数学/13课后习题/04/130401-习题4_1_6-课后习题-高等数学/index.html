习题4-1

2 求下列不定积分

（16）$\int{3^x e^x}dx$
$$
解：\
\int{3^x e^x}dx=\int{(3e)^x}dx\
=\frac{(3e)^x}{\ln(3e)}+C=\frac{3^xe^x}{1+\ln3}+C
$$
(18)$\int{\sec x(\sec x-\tan x)}dx$
$$
解：\
\int{\sec x(\sec x-\tan x)}dx=\int{\sec^2 x}dx - \int{\sec x\tan x}dx\
=\tan x +\sec x +C
$$
(19) $\int{\cos^2 \frac{x}{2}}dx$
$$
解：\
\int{\cos^2 \frac{x}{2}}dx=\int{\frac{1+\cos x}{2}}dx \
=\frac{x+\sin x}{2}+C
$$

习题4-2

2 求下列不定积分（其中$a,b,\omega, \phi$均为常数)

（12）$\int{\cos^2(\omega t+\phi)\sin(\omega t+\phi)}dt$
$$
解:\
\int{\cos^2(\omega t+\phi)\sin(\omega t+\phi)}dt\
=-\frac{1}{\omega}\int{\cos^2(\omega t+\phi)}d\cos(\omega t+\phi)\
=-\frac{1}{3\omega}\cos^2(\omega t+\phi)+C
$$
(15)$\int{\tan^{10}x\sec^2xdx}$​
$$
解:\
\int{\tan^{10}x\sec^2xdx}=\int{\tan^{10}xd\tan x}\
=\frac{1}{11}\tan^{11}x+C
$$
（22）$\int{\frac{dx}{\sin x\cos x}}$
$$
解:\
\int{\frac{dx}{\sin x\cos x}}=\int{\frac{d2x}{\sin 2x}}\
=\int{\csc 2x}d2x=\ln|\csc 2x-\cos 2x|+C
$$
（26）$\int{\sin 2x\cos 3x}dx$​
$$
解：\
\int{\sin 2x\cos 3x}dx=\frac{1}{2}\int{(\sin5x-\sin x)}dx\
=-\frac{\cos5x}{10}-\frac{\cos x}{2}+C
$$
（34）$\int{\frac{dx}{(x+1)(x-2)}}$
$$
解:\
\int{\frac{dx}{(x+1)(x-2)}}=\frac{1}{-3}\int{(\frac{1}{x+1}-\frac{1}{x-2})dx}\
=-\frac{1}{3}\ln|\frac{x+1}{x-2}|+C
$$

习题 4-3

就下列不定积分

（3）$\int{\arcsin xdx}$
$$
解：\
\int{\arcsin xdx} = x\arcsin x-\int{xd\arcsin x}\
=x\arcsin x-\int{\frac{x}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}}dx\
=x\arcsin x+\sqrt{1-x^2}+C
$$
（5）$\int{x^2\ln x}dx$
$$
解：\
\int{x^2\ln x}dx=\frac{1}{3}\int{\ln xdx^3}\
=\frac{1}{3}x^3\ln x-\frac{1}{3}\int{x^3d\ln x}\
=\frac{1}{3}x^3\ln x-\frac{1}{3}\int{x^2}dx\
=\frac{1}{9}x^3(3\ln x-1)+C
$$
（19）$\int{e^{\sqrt[3]{x}}dx}$
$$
解：\
令\sqrt[3]{x}=t\
x=t^3,dx=dt^3=3t^2dt\
\int{e^{\sqrt[3]{x}}dx}=\int{e^t\cdot 3t^2dt}\
=3\int{t^2de^t}\
=3(t^2e^t-\int{e^tdt^2})\
=3t^2e^t-6\int{te^tdt}\
=3t^2e^t-6\int{tde^t}\
=3t^2e^t-6(te^t-\int{e^tdt})\
=3t^2e^t-6te^t+6e^t+C=3e^t(t^2-2t+2)+C\
回带x，\int{e^{\sqrt[3]{x}}dx}=3e^{\sqrt[3]{x}}(\sqrt[3]{x^2}-2\sqrt[3]{x}+2)+C
$$

习题 4-4

求下列不定积分

（3）$\int{\frac{x+1}{x^2-2x+5}dx}$
$$
解：\
\int{\frac{x+1}{x^2-2x+5}dx}=\int{\frac{x+1}{(x-1)^2+2^2}dx}\
=\int{\frac{x-1}{(x-1)^2+2^2}d(x-1)}+\int{\frac{2}{(x-1)^2+2^2}d(x-1)}\
=\frac{1}{2}\ln(x^2-2x+5)+\arctan\frac{x-1}{2}+C
$$
（4）$\int{\frac{dx}{x(x^2+1)}}$
$$
解:\
\int{\frac{dx}{x(x^2+1)}}=\int{\frac{1+x^2-x^2}{x(x^2+1)}dx}\
=\int{\frac{1}{x}dx}-\int{\frac{x}{(x^2+1)}dx}\
=\ln|x|-\ln(x^2+1)+C
$$
（9）$\int{\frac{dx}{(x^2+1)(x^2+x)}}$
$$
解：\
\int{\frac{dx}{(x^2+1)(x^2+x)}}=\int{\frac{dx}{x(x+1)(x^2+1)}}\
=\int{(-\frac{1}{2}x)dx}+\int{(-\frac{1}{2(x+1)})dx}+\int{\frac{-\frac{1}{2}x-\frac{1}{2}}{x^2+1}dx}\
=-\frac{1}{4}x^2-\frac{1}{2}\ln|x+1|-\frac{1}{2}\int{\frac{x+1}{x^2+1}dx}\
=-\frac{1}{2}x^2-\frac{1}{2}\ln|x+1|-\frac{1}{4}\ln(x^2+1)-\frac{1}{2}\arctan x+C
$$
（14）$\int{\frac{dx}{3+\sin^2x}}$
$$
解：\
令u=\tan\frac{x}{2},dx=\frac{2}{1+u^2}du\
原积分公式=\int{\frac{1}{3+(\frac{2u}{1+u^2})^2}}\cdot \frac{2}{1+u^2}du\
=\int{\frac{(1+u^2)^2}{4u^2+3(1+u^2)^2}\cdot\frac{2}{1+u^2} du}\
=\int{\frac{2u^2+2}{3u^4+10u^2+3}du}\
=\frac{1}{2}\int{\frac{1}{3u^2+1}du}+\frac{1}{2}\int{\frac{1}{u^2+3}du}\
=\frac{1}{2}\arctan(\sqrt{3}u)+\frac{\sqrt{3}}{6}\arctan\frac{\sqrt{3}u}{3}+C\

把u=\tan\frac{x}{2}带入上式，\
原积分公式=\frac{1}{2}\arctan(\sqrt{3}\tan\frac{x}{2})+\frac{\sqrt{3}}{6}\arctan\frac{\sqrt{3}x}{6}+C
$$
（24）$\int{\frac{dx}{\sqrt[3]{(x+1)^2(x-1)^4}}}$
$$
解：\
\int{\frac{dx}{\sqrt[3]{(x+1)^2(x-1)^4}}}\
=\int{\frac{dx}{\sqrt[3]{(x+1)^3(x-1)^3\frac{x-1}{x+1}}}}\
=\int{\frac{1}{(x+1)(x-1)\sqrt[3]{\frac{x-1}{x+1}}}dx}\
令\sqrt[3]{\frac{x-1}{x+1}}=t,dx= -6\frac{t^2}{(t^3-1)^2}dt\
原积分公式=\int{\frac{1}{-\frac{2}{t^3-1}\cdot(-\frac{2t^3}{t^3-1})t}\cdot (-6\frac{t^2}{(t^3-1)^2})dt}\
=-\frac{3}{2}\int{\frac{1}{t^2}dt}\
=\frac{3}{2}\frac{1}{t}+C\
把t=\sqrt[3]{\frac{x-1}{x+1}}带入上式\
原积分公式=\frac{3}{2}\sqrt[3]{\frac{x-1}{x+1}}+C
$$
